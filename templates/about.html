About Yatrik

On a mission to make intercity bus travel safer, faster, and transparent for everyone in India.

Our Story

Yatrik began with a simple idea — you should always know where your bus is and when it will arrive. What started as a tool to help friends coordinate long-distance trips has grown into a platform serving thousands of routes across India.

Today, we partner with verified operators and leverage modern technology to provide reliable real-time information so you can travel with confidence.
